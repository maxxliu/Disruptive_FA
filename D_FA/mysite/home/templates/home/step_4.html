Step 4: Calculate the Discount Rate

 

Capital Structure:

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

    We will use WACC as our value for the discount rate
    WACC = (E / D+E) * (re) + (D / D+E) * (rd)(1 - t)
    Where:
      E = market value of equity (Cost of Equity)
      D = market value of debt (Borrowing Rate)
      re = cost of equity
      rd = cost of debt
      t = corporate tax rate

 

Cost of Equity

Beta= measure of how volatile the company is relative to the overall market

rRF = Risk-free rate (usually 10 year Treasury rate)

rM - rRF = Equity market risk premium (threshold for risk)

    This is the return investors expect over the risk-free rate for the volatility of the stock
    We will modify this value with the results from our risk assessment survey in our Advanced Search

Cost of Equity = rRF + Beta(rM - rRF)
