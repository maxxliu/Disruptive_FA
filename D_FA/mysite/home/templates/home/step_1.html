Step 1: Establish a Timeframe

 

Determine the time period for your FCF projection

5 Year Projection:

    Less predictable cash flows, riskier company
    Company still has a decent market position, may provide some competitive advantages such as patents, brand names

10 Year Projection:

    Stable, predictable cash flows
    Dominant market position, high barriers to entry in the industry

 

It becomes increasingly difficult to project FCF further into the future and increasingly difficult to justify your assumptions

Our application uses 5 Year Projections

    Real world companies rarely meet the requirements for a reliable 10 Year Projection
    We live in a volatile world and would hate to be the reason for the loss of your life savings :)
