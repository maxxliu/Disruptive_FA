Step 6: Calculate Terminal Value

 

Terminal Value is the value of the business beyond the projected timeline

Projecting into the future can be impractical

 

Two Methods of Calculation

Gordon Growth Model

    AKA perpetuity growth method
    Assumes FCFs grow at a constant rate forever
      Terminal Value = FCFn*(1+g) / (r-g)
      Where: r = WACC, g = growth rate, n = final year of projection

Exit Multiple Method

    Values business as a multiple of operating statistic
      Terminal Value = EBITDAn * Multiple
